Why Australia

Australia has long been the popular overseas study destinations. Australia is a safe, friendly, sophisticated and harmonious society in which students can learn and travel. With only the US and UK earlier than it, Australia has the third largest number of international students studying there. For several students from everywhere in the world, Australia is their first choice of English-speaking study destination. This can be actually because Australian qualifications are world recognized and sometimes lead to good job positions. The study costs are also just like the US & UK. Australian degrees are perceived worldwide, both in terms of as a base for pursuing every kind of education also as securing jobs. This can be actually because of the Australian Qualifications Framework (AQF), which could be a national system of learning pathways recognized throughout Australia and therefore the world.

                         

The Education Services for Overseas Students (ESOS) Framework covers the availability of education services to overseas students. This legal framework sets out the requirements for registration as an ESOS provider. It also defines the standards that providers offering courses to foreign students must meet. The framework provides a regular national approach, which helps ensure high-quality training and high standards of take care for international students. ASQA monitors and assesses Commonwealth Register of Institutions and Courses for Overseas Students (CRICOS) providers' compliance with the ESOS Framework.
