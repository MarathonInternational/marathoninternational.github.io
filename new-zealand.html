Tuition fee & living expenses

When calculating the annual cost of a replacement Zealand education at a selected college or university, add a minimum of NZ$ 16,000 Approx. to the published cost of tuition, fees, and room and board. The University Board publishes program costs, required admissions tests, and other useful information within the International Student Handbook of latest Zealand Universities. When preparing a budget, you will need to account for the following costs:

  • Application fee ranges from 0 to NZ$ 250 per school.
  • Academic entrance examinations will cost you around $150 to $200 per examination, and most students take at least 2 tests. Assume that you will be spending at least $500 on tests.
  • Tuition and fees will cost between NZ$ 22,000 and NZ$35,000 approx. per academic year, depending on the type and quality of institution.
  • Books and study materials will cost NZ$ 700 to NZ$ 1000 approx. per academic year.
  • Travel costs to the New Zealand vary, but will be between NZ$ 900 and NZ$ 1500 approx. Travel costs within the Australia will be between NZ$ 150 and NZ$ 300 approx.
  • Room and board will cost between NZ$ 6,500 and NZ$ 14,400 approx. annually. Allow an extra NZ$ 1,000 to NZ$ 2,000 for vacation periods when the dormitory might be closed.
  • If you will be living off campus, the cost of renting an apartment will be between NZ$ 350 and NZ$ 600 a month but could be much higher contingent on the location of the school. Meals should cost about NZ$ 2800 a year, assuming that you do not eat in restaurants too many times.
  • Clothing will cost NZ$ 1100 or more annually.
  • Health insurance will cost NZ$ 700 - NZ$ 1700 for two years.
  • Personal expenses will cost around NZ$ 2000 approx. per year.
  • If you bring other family members with you, assume that your annual expenses will increase about 20% or NZ$ 8000 approx. for each additional family member.
  • If you wish to buy a car, assume that it will cost you around NZ$ 1800 a year.
  • If you will be traveling during the summer, assume a cost of NZ$ 50 to NZ$ 110 approx. a day for touring. If you may be continuing your education during the summer, add half the figure you calculated for the total year.
  • Assume that your costs will increase by about 5% per year due to inflation. Do not forget to include an allowance of about 10% to account for changes in exchange rates.
