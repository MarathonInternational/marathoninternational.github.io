PTE (A)–PEARSON TEST OF ENGLISH (ACADEMIC)

Official web link for PTE - www.pearsonpte.com


What is PTE (A)?

PTE Academic is a computer-based English language test for non-native speakers who want to study at institutions where English is the main language of instruction.

The test will accurately measure your Listening, Reading, Speaking and Writing abilities with question types that reflect how language is actually used in an academic environment. Test takers find PTE Academic an interesting and remarkable way to demonstrate their English language skills to international institutions. PTE is graded on a scale of 90.

Pearson Language Tests is a unit of the Pearson PLC group, dedicated to assessing and validating the English language usage of non-native English speakers.

Why PTE test?

If you are looking to work, live or study in an English-speaking country, then you need to demonstrate a high level of English language ability.

English is the third most spoken language in the world, with 379 million speakers worldwide.


Few benefits with PTE test:

  • PTE is Widely accepted by universities, colleges and governments around the world.
  • Results typically available within just 48 hours of taking the test.
  • Computer-based testing and scoring means you are assessed objectively and consistently.

PTE test is globallyrecognized by thousands of universities worldwide, with the most popular being the UK, Australia, the US and Canada.
