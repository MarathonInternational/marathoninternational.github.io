Migrate to Australia



Australia is well known for its “no Class system” unlike other nations. Citizens are by and large very friendly and inviting, and cultivate a feeling of local area. The Country is made up of multiple cultures from different nations. Although many nations worldwide have faced recession at certain point of time but, since 25 years Australia has never faced recession due to reforms and stood inspiration to the world.

Australia provides numerous career opportunities for the immigrants because of its rapid economic growth and expanding business ventures. The country has a well-built healthcare system that supports medical payments and hospitalisation facilities for not only citizens but also international students and immigrants.

Despite the fact that it is away from rest of the nations, Australia is thought of by many immigrants to be probably the best country to work and live. It is a place that is known for people with different cultures, wildlife, beautiful beaches, amazing landscapes and a place of nature lovers. Additionally, it is the ideal place for young and well educated couples intending to begin their new life and career together.

Entry Requirements

  1. Points for any visa categories are awarded based on selection factors like age, education, work experience, language proficiency, employment and adaptability.
  2. Minimum three years of work experience in the last ten years in a skilled occupation list (Medium- and long-term Strategic Skills List / Short-term Skilled Occupation List  /Regional Occupations List).
  3. Under English languageproficiency, minimum of competent level is required.
  4. If the candidate has foreign education (outside of Australia), the degree must be from a recognized institution.
  5. Have to provide proof of funds to show that candidates will be able to support themselves and accompanying family members financially for their move to Australia.
  6. Candidate must be admissible to Australia.
