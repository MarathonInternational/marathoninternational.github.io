TOEFL(TEST OF ENGLISH AS A FOREIGN LANGUAGE) – IBT (INTERNET BASED TEST)

Official web link for TOEFL - www.ets.org/toefl



What is TOEFL?

TOEFL is one among the foremost popular English proficiency exams accepted at American and Canadian universities and schools abroad. It assesses your ability to both speak and understand English by analysing your English ability in terms of reading, speaking, listening and writing.

The test will accurately measure your Listening, Reading, Speaking and Writing abilities with question types that reflect how language is actually used in an academic environment. Test takers find TOEFL an interesting and remarkable way to demonstrate their English language skills to international institutions. TOEFL is graded on a scale of 120.

TOEFL is a unit of ETS, dedicated to assessing and validating the English language usage of non-native English speakers.

Why TOEFL test?

If you are looking to work, live or study in an English-speaking country, then you must be able to demonstrate a high level of English language ability.

TOEFL is recognized by more than 11,000 universities and other institutions in over 150countries, including Australia, New Zealand, Canada, USA, UK and across Europe and Asia.

The questions in TOEFL are straight forward and is found to be easier to get higher scores. TOEFL is little less expensive than other two tests (IELTS & PTE).

Types:


  1. TOEFLIBT
  2. TOEFL PBT

Modules in TOEFL:


  1. Reading
  2. Listening
  3. Speaking
  4. Writing

Benefits of a high TOEFL score:


  • The TOEFL iBT test measures your ability to form complex ideas and confidently express them in English — skills you’ll need to succeed in an academic university environment.
  • Visa Officers (VO) also give weightage to high TOEFL scores while examining visa applications.
  • Chances of getting Scholarships / Financial Aid are brighter with a high TOEFL score.

Which country needs TOEFL:

Almost all English-speaking countries

  • Total marks: TOEFL is conducted for 120 marks
  • Test Fee: Approx. Rs.14,200/- (for India)
  • Test Duration:3hrs 20 mins.
  • Test Validity: 2 years
